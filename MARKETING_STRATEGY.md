# MB AI Group — Marketing Strategy for mbai-group.com

**Positioning:** "Frontier AI, applied to MENA." You are not a freelancer with a portfolio — you are a founder running a group of AI ventures, credentialed by frontier-lab experience (former xAI Human Data Lead). Every marketing move should reinforce that founder-of-a-group framing.

---

## 1. The core narrative

One sentence, everywhere (LinkedIn bio, X bio, email signature, pitch decks):

> "Former xAI Human Data Lead building MB AI Group — AutoLeads, Virlo, IBNI, and TUT — frontier-grade AI for the markets the frontier forgot."

Why it works: credential (xAI) → ambition (group of ventures) → mission (MENA gets frontier AI). It invites the question "what's the group?" — which the website now answers.

## 2. Audience segments & what each should see

| Segment | What they care about | Entry point |
|---|---|---|
| UAE real-estate brokers & MENA SMB owners | Booked appointments, not tech | AutoLeads + testimonials + Calendly CTA |
| Fintech / enterprise decision-makers | Rigour, grounded AI, compliance | Market Sentinel demo + xAI pedigree section |
| Investors & partners | Vision, execution velocity, market size | Ventures section (4 ventures, 1 live) + career page |
| Recruiters / collaborators / press | The story | Our Story page + CV download |

## 3. Channel plan (90 days)

**Weeks 1–2 — Foundation**
- Point mbai-group.com at the Vercel deployment (DNS: A record `76.76.21.21` or CNAME `cname.vercel-dns.com`).
- Set up Google Search Console + submit sitemap; install Plausible or GA4.
- Rewrite LinkedIn and X profiles with the core narrative; link the site.
- Publish the site announcement post on LinkedIn ("I rebuilt my company's home — here's what MB AI Group is building").

**Weeks 3–6 — Proof content (2 posts/week)**
- LinkedIn is your primary channel — MENA B2B lives there.
- Alternate two formats: (a) *Build logs* — short posts showing a real feature or demo (Virlo trend radar, IBNI build animation, screen recordings from the site); (b) *Insider lessons* — "What working on frontier-model data taught me about X". These compound your credential.
- Every post ends with one CTA: the site or the Calendly link. Never both.

**Weeks 7–12 — Distribution & lead capture**
- Short-form video: 30–60s screen-capture demos of each venture (Virlo/IBNI/TUT pages are designed to demo well). Post to LinkedIn, X, Instagram Reels — this is Virlo's own thesis; use the tool's framing publicly ("we scored this trend 94").
- Cold outreach for AutoLeads: 20 personalized messages/week to UAE brokerage owners, each linking to a specific demo on the site rather than a generic pitch.
- Guest appearances: MENA tech podcasts and newsletters (e.g., regional startup media) pitching the "xAI → MENA founder" story.

## 4. Website conversion loop
- Primary CTA everywhere: **Book a 30-min Strategy Call** (already wired to Calendly).
- Secondary CTA: venture "early access" — add an email-capture form later for Virlo/IBNI/TUT waitlists (turns curiosity into a launch list; aim for 500 emails before Virlo launches).
- Each venture launch then has a built-in audience: waitlist → launch post → case studies.

## 5. SEO / AEO quick wins (already partly done in the rebuild)
- Meta/OG tags for mbai-group.com are in place; add per-page titles for /virlo, /ibni, /tut when they go live.
- Target keywords: "AI lead generation UAE", "AI agency Dubai", "Arabic NLP", "AI app builder Arabic". Write one landing-page-quality blog post per keyword.
- Get listed: Clutch, GoodFirms, MENA startup directories — backlinks + social proof.

## 6. Measurement
- North star: booked strategy calls/week.
- Supporting: site visits → Calendly clicks (target 3–5%), waitlist signups, LinkedIn follower growth.
- Review every 2 weeks; double down on the post format that drives calls, kill the rest.

## 7. Launch checklist for each new venture (Virlo → IBNI → TUT)
1. Swap the placeholder page for the real product (the route and design are already in place).
2. Announce to the waitlist first, then LinkedIn/X the next day.
3. Publish one case study within 30 days of launch.
4. Update the Ventures section status badge from "In Development" to "Live" — the site is designed so this is a one-line change.
